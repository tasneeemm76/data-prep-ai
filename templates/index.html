Welcome to the Data Preprocessing App!
This tool helps you clean and prepare your dataset for further analysis or machine learning models. Simply upload your CSV file, and the app will automatically handle tasks like:

  • Identifying and filling missing values
  • Encoding categorical data
  • Removing duplicate rows
  • Scaling numeric features
  • Handling inconsistent data types

After processing, you can download the cleaned dataset and use it for further analysis. Additionally, you can visit the "More Pre-processing" page for advanced AI-driven preprocessing options.
